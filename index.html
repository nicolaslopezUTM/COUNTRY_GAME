Nicolas Lopez GIS Specialist

Nicolas Lopez is a GIS Analyst passionate about geo-data, scripting and remote sensing techonologies.

With a background in Environmental Science and Ecology with his BSc in Zoology, Nicolas quickly become passionate about GIS and data analysis. He later specialised with a MSc in GIS.

He now has over a year experience as a GIS Analyst in the Energy Industry. Dealing with a variety of geospatial task, from automation and data-management to Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis and Routing. He is a geospatial python specialist with numerous development of integrated ArcGIS Pro techonologies as well as scripts using libraries like geopandas and rasterio.
